Frequently asked questions

What is Let Me ChatGPT That For You?

It's LMGTFY for the AI age. Someone asked you a question an AI could answer in seconds, so you create a link that types it into the AI for them — slowly, theatrically, and with just a hint of judgment.

Which AIs are supported?

Six of them: ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Grok, Microsoft Copilot, and Google AI Mode. Your link replays the question in a faithful clone of whichever one you pick, then hands them over to the real thing.

Is it free?

Completely free. No account, no sign-up, no premium tier. The only cost is the small dent in your friend's pride.

Why did someone send me this link?

Because you asked them something an AI could have answered instantly. Watch the little demo, take the hint gracefully, and next time type the question into the chatbot yourself.

How do I send someone a ChatGPT link that asks their question?

Type their question into the box above, pick ChatGPT (or any of the other five AIs), and hit Get Link. You get a shareable link that opens a fake chat, types their question letter by letter, then hands them to the real ChatGPT with the answer already loading. Copy, paste, send, enjoy the silence.

What is the LMGTFY for AI / ChatGPT?

LMGTFY — Let Me Google That For You — was the classic sarcastic search link for lazy questions. This is the same idea upgraded for the chatbot era: a let-me-ChatGPT-that-for-you link generator that works with ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Grok, Copilot, and Google AI Mode. Same passive aggression, newer technology.
